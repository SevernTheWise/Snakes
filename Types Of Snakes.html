Discover the World of Snakes

Explore the fascinating world of snakes, from the deadly cobra to the powerful boa constrictor.

Python (Morelia viridis)

Large constrictor snake found in Asia, Africa, and Australia.

Python's are known for their immense size and constricting hunting style. They typically ambush their prey, using their muscular bodies to suffocate it before swallowing it whole. Some species, like the reticulated python, can grow to over 30 feet in length.

Next

Cobra

Famous for its hood, cobras are venomous and highly defensive.

Cobras are known for their characteristic hood, which they flare when threatened. Most species of cobras are highly venomous, using their poison to immobilize and kill prey. They can be found in Africa and Asia and are often revered in mythology.

Viper

Highly venomous with long fangs, vipers are found worldwide.

Vipers are a diverse family of snakes known for their long, hinged fangs that allow them to inject venom deep into their prey. They are found in a wide range of habitats from deserts to tropical forests and are often very aggressive when threatened.

Boa Constrictor

Famous for its large size and constriction technique.

The Boa Constrictor is a formidable snake, which crushes the life out of the prey it hunts. Boa Constrictors are not known to be aggressive, but if threatened, they can turn deadly.
